The Retreat Experience

What is the Varsha Retreat?

Rooted in somatic awareness and Compassionate Inquiry®, this retreat invites you to explore the stories, emotions, and patterns that shape your inner world—at your own pace, in a space that feels safe and supported.

You will be guided through practices that will help you:

  • Develop a deeper connection with your body
  • Understand emotional patterns with clarity
  • Build capacity to stay present with your experience
  • Cultivate a more compassionate relationship with yourself
